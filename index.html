About Random Shayari Generator

Random Shayari Generator is a web application that generates random Hindi shayari (poetry) with just a click of a button. It provides a beautiful and user-friendly interface to display the shayari along with the name of the poet.

You can easily copy the shayari text, share it on Twitter, or even listen to an audio playback of the shayari. Additionally, there are various controls available to navigate between different shayaris, download the audio quote, toggle the background image, and download a screenshot of the quote.

The application also offers sharing options, allowing you to copy the link to the current shayari and share it on various social media platforms with just a single click.

Random Shayari Generator is designed to provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for anyone who loves Hindi poetry and wants to discover new shayaris with ease.
